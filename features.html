Comprehensive Fleet Management Tools

Our platform delivers the advanced features required to maximize fleet efficiency, reduce operational costs, and ensure regulatory compliance.

Real-Time GPS & Mapping

Instantly view the precise location of every vehicle and asset on a single, dynamic map. Utilize historical route playback and geo-fencing for control.

Driver Behavior Monitoring

Receive reports on harsh acceleration, aggressive braking, and speeding incidents. Use scorecards to incentivize safer and more fuel-efficient driving.

Temperature and Sensor Alerts

Crucial for refrigerated transport and specialized assets. Set custom temperature ranges and receive instant alerts when thresholds are breached.

Automated Maintenance Scheduling

Track vehicle odometer readings, engine hours, and DTC (Diagnostic Trouble Codes) to automate service reminders and preventative maintenance.

Compliance and Reporting

Generate detailed, customizable reports on working hours, idle time, and activity history, simplifying regulatory compliance and audits.

Vehicle Security & Immobilization

Protect your assets with remote ignition disable capabilities and tamper alerts, drastically improving the security and recovery process.
